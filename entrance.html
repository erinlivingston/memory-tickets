Enter Select Ticket About Microarchive Color

Tickets are scraps of our movement & time, and our transactions & consumption. Once a material piece of travel memory, now, part of digital fabrics that form global infrastructures & interfaces. This project plays with digital ticketing from commuter rail and explores a collection of ticket screenshots as mnemonic objects. Enter the train to subvert color and time, and to consider an embodied digital travel memory.

--/--/---- --:--:--

Trains have a form of time all their own. In physics, trains are a crucial example of Relative Time, a part of Einstein's theory of relativity. It means, simply put, that time depends on our movement in & through space.
